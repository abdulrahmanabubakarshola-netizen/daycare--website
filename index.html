About Us

For over 10 years, Little Sprouts has been a trusted home-away-from-home for families. We combine play, learning, and care to support each child's emotional, social, and intellectual growth.

Our certified caregivers create a warm, colorful, and stimulating environment where children feel safe to explore, imagine, and make friends.
